Creamy Vodka Pasta

This simple, easy to make pasta is delicious, creamy, and has a nice little kick.

By Finch

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Penne Pasta
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Red Pepper Flake
  • 1/2 Tsp Oregano
  • Olive Oil to taste
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Tube Tomato Paste
  • 1/3 Cup Vodka
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Reggiano until happy

Directions

  1. In a pan, heat olive oil.
  2. Heat spices in oil for one minute.
  3. Add garlic and tomato paste.
  4. Saut√© until garlic becomes brown.
  5. Add vodka and heavy cream and mix well.
  6. Turn heat to low and let simmer.
  7. In a large pot, boil pasta to al dente.
  8. Reserve one cup pasta water and drain pasta.
  9. Combine pasta, sauce, and pasta water, heating to simmer.
  10. Add Parmesan Reggiano to desired level.
