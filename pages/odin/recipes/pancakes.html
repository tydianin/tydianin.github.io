Super Fluffy Pancakes

These vegetarian pancakes are simple to make and feel like munching on a cloud.

By Hannah

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup All-Purpose Flour, sifted
  • 2 Tbsp White Sugar
  • 1 Pinch Salt
  • 2 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Large Egg, beaten
  • 3/4 Cup Milk
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Tbsp Unsalted Butter, melted
  • Canola Oil as needed

Directions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder into a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the egg, milk, vanilla, and melted butter.
    • Make sure your butter is not hot, or it will melt the eggs.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then stir.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over low-medium heat and coat with oil.
  5. Use a 1/4 cup measuring spoon to scoop the batter into the pan.
  6. Cook until each side is golden brown.
  7. Set aside in a warm container until all batter is used.
